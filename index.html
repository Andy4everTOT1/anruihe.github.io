About

I am an MS student at the National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by Prof. Mike Zheng Shou. Prior to NUS, I received my B.E. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University, where I was advised by Prof. Juncheng Li.


My research lies at the intersection of embodied AI, multimodal large language models, and 3D scene understanding. I am passionate about building AI systems that can perceive, reason about, and act in the physical world. I have co-authored papers on mitigating hallucinations in LVLMs and developing real-time scene understanding systems integrated into wearable smart-vision aids.

简介

我是新加坡国立大学（NUS）的研究生，师从 Mike Zheng Shou 教授。 此前，我在浙江大学获得计算机科学学士学位，师从李俊成教授。


我的研究方向涉及具身智能、多模态大语言模型以及3D场景理解等领域。 我热衷于构建能够感知、推理并与物理世界交互的 AI 系统。 我曾参与论文发表，研究方向包括减轻多模态大模型中的幻觉现象，以及开发集成于智能穿戴设备中的实时场景理解系统。
